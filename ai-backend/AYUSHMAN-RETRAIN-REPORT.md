### 2️⃣ What is Ayushman Bharat Yojana? (English)

**Enhanced Answer (602 chars):**
```
Ayushman Bharat Yojana (Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana - PMJAY) is 
India's largest health insurance scheme launched in September 2018. It 
provides eligible families with free medical treatment up to 5 lakh 
rupees per year. This scheme is designed for poor and middle class 
families. It covers 1,350+ medical procedures including surgery, 
medicines, diagnostic tests, hospitalization, and follow-up care. You 
can get cashless treatment at any empanelled hospital across India. The 
scheme covers more than 10 crore families (50+ crore people). It is the 
world's largest government funded health scheme.
```
