### 2️⃣ What is Ayushman Bharat Yojana? (English)

**Answer:**
```
Ayushman Bharat Yojana (Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana) is a government 
health insurance scheme launched in September 2018. It provides eligible 
families with free medical treatment up to 5 lakh rupees per year. This 
scheme is for poor and middle class families. It covers 1,350+ procedures 
including surgery, medicines, diagnostic tests, and hospitalization. You 
can get treatment at any empanelled hospital. The scheme covers more than 
10 crore families.
```

### 3️⃣ Ayushman card kaise banwaye? (Hinglish)

**Answer:**
```
Ayushman card banwane ke liye yeh steps follow karein:

   sakte hain
```

### 4️⃣ How to make Ayushman card? (English)

**Answer:**
```
To make Ayushman card, follow these steps:

1. First check your eligibility - Visit Ayushman Bharat website 
   (pmjay.gov.in) and enter your mobile number and Aadhaar/Ration card 
   number

2. If you are eligible, visit nearest Common Service Center (CSC) or 
   empanelled hospital

3. Take your documents - Aadhaar card, Ration card, and address proof

4. CSC operator will print your card instantly

5. Card is completely free, no charges

6. After getting card, you can get treatment at any empanelled hospital
```

### 5️⃣ Ayushman card ke liye eligible kaun hai? (Hinglish)

**Answer:**
```
Ayushman Bharat scheme me yeh log eligible hain:

1. SECC 2011 (Socio-Economic Caste Census) data me listed families

2. Garib aur deprived families jo specific criteria meet karte hain

3. Rural areas me: Kaccha ghar, no adult member (16-59 years), female 
   headed household, disabled member, SC/ST families, landless laborers

4. Urban areas me: Rag pickers, beggars, domestic workers, street vendors, 
   construction workers, etc.

5. Eligibility check karne ke liye pmjay.gov.in par jaayein ya toll-free 
   number 14555 par call karein

6. Agar aapka naam SECC list me hai to aap automatically eligible hain
```

### 6️⃣ Who is eligible for Ayushman card? (English)

**Answer:**
```
Following people are eligible for Ayushman Bharat scheme:

1. Families listed in SECC 2011 (Socio-Economic Caste Census) data

2. Poor and deprived families meeting specific criteria

3. In rural areas: Kaccha house, no adult member (16-59 years), female 
   headed household, disabled member, SC/ST families, landless laborers

4. In urban areas: Rag pickers, beggars, domestic workers, street vendors, 
   construction workers, etc.

5. To check eligibility, visit pmjay.gov.in or call toll-free number 14555

6. If your name is in SECC list, you are automatically eligible
```
